# Quick Voice Intro Recording Checklist

## Before Recording
- [ ] Find a quiet room
- [ ] Test your microphone
- [ ] Write your script
- [ ] Practice reading it a few times

## Recording
- [ ] Record your voice intro (keep it under 30 seconds)
- [ ] Speak clearly and at a comfortable pace
- [ ] Record multiple takes and choose the best one

## Editing
- [ ] Remove background noise
- [ ] Normalize audio levels (-3dB to -6dB)
- [ ] Trim silence from beginning/end
- [ ] Export as MP3 (128kbps or 192kbps)

## Uploading
- [ ] Go to `/admin` in your portfolio
- [ ] Navigate to Site Settings
- [ ] Upload voice intro file
- [ ] Publish changes

## Testing
- [ ] Refresh portfolio homepage
- [ ] Click audio button
- [ ] Verify voice intro plays
- [ ] Check audio quality and volume

## Recommended Tools
- **Recording**: Audacity (free), Voice Memos (mobile)
- **Editing**: Audacity, GarageBand
- **Online**: online-voice-recorder.com
